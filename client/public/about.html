About Me

I am currently studying towards an MEng Computer Science degree and am set to graduate in July 2021 with a 2:1 honours degree from the University of Portsmouth. My aspirations are to secure a Graduate Data Analyst position or similar to utilise my analytical skills and knowledge that I have gained in the area during my studies and work experiences.

Born in Canada in 1997, I moved to the UK at the age of 7 and have lived the majority of my life here. I attended Tomlinscote School and Sixth Form College. At A-levels I studied Maths, Physics and Business and have always been massively interested and motivated by numerical probelm solving. I took this with me to University, starting off studying Mechanical Engineering but switching to BSc Computer Science after discovering my passion for computer coding and computers in general.

My time at University taught me just as much about being a functioning adult as it did about computer science. During A-levels and my first year at Uni I was certainly not prepared for the self-discipline and independence that is required to succeed at University. However, I have become excellent organisationally, meeting deadlines and being pro-active about completing tasks. I truly believe that these are the most important skills I have learned from University because a person can be taught to understand complicated algorithms but it is much harder for a person to break out of bad habits.
